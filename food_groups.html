Essential Nutrients Provided by Food Groups

Food groups are categories of foods that provide the essential nutrients our bodies need to function properly. These include grains, vegetables, fruits, dairy, and protein. To maintain good health, it's important to have a healthy amount of different foods from each of these groups. Each food group offers a unique set of vitamins, minerals, and other nutrients that our bodies require. For example, whole grains are a great source of fiber, vitamins, and minerals, while vegetables are low in calories but high in vitamins and antioxidants. Fruits are also high in vitamins and antioxidants, while dairy products are an important source of calcium and protein. Protein-rich foods, such as lean meats, fish, eggs, and beans, provide the building blocks our bodies need to repair and maintain tissue. By including a variety of foods from each food group in our diets, we can ensure that we are getting all the nutrients we need to stay healthy.
